How to Play Android Flappy

Welcome to Android Flappy! Follow these instructions to start playing:

    Press Pause to start.
  1. Press the "Up" button or the spacebar to make the bird flap its wings and fly upward.
  2. Avoid crashing into the green pipes.
  3. Pass through the gaps between the pipes to earn points.
  4. Keep flying and scoring as many points as you can!
